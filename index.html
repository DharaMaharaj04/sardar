Statue of Unity

A Monumental Tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Who Was Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel?

Known as the “Iron Man of India”, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was India’s first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister. He played a pivotal role in integrating 562 princely states into the Indian Union post-independence.

  • Symbol of Unity, Strength, and Leadership
  • Visionary and Strategic Administrator
  • Architect of Modern India's Political Unification

Architectural Highlights

  • Walking stance posture – symbol of leadership
  • Bronze alloy cladding assembled in China, mounted in India
  • Wind and earthquake resistant design (up to 180 km/h)
  • Viewing gallery at 153 meters offers panoramic views
